About

Yummy Tummy AI helps you turn the ingredients you have into dinner ideas - via a simple, private chat.

Responsive design: It works on phones and desktop today; we’re still polishing layout and interactions.

What is it?

A lightweight web chat that suggests recipes, ingredient swaps, and step-by-step guidance based on what you type. No account required today. Account login and cloud sync are planned.

How it works

  • Type or paste what you’ve got and what you feel like cooking.
  • Your message is sent securely to an AI service and the reply is sent back to the site.
  • Saved chats are currently local to your browser; account-based cloud storage is planned.

Privacy

Local storage

Saved chats are currently stored in localStorage on this device (e.g., key yt_ai_draft for drafts). Clearing your browser data removes them. The live conversation is held temporarily in memory on the server to keep context. Account-based cloud history is planned.

What’s sent

When you press Send, your message is processed by an AI service over HTTPS. This server keeps a short in-memory session history for context and does not persist it to a database.

Tip: avoid sharing sensitive personal or payment information in any AI chat.

Current limitations

  • Image attachments are not processed yet (text-only responses).
  • Responsive design is being refined; behavior may vary by device/browser.
  • Suggestions are informational - always check allergies and doneness to your preference.

FAQ

Where are my chats stored? ⌄

Saved chats are currently in your browser’s local storage on this device. Account and cloud chat history support is planned.

Does it support photos? ⌄

Not yet. You can attach images, but they are currently ignored.

Is it free? ⌄

The site itself doesn’t charge. If a third-party AI service is used, usage may incur costs under that provider.

Why are assistant messages on the left? ⌄

To match common messenger patterns: assistant (left), you (right). The landing page preview mirrors the chat.

Roadmap

  • Better saved-chat management and export.
  • Optional offline/PWA support.

Subject to change based on feedback.
