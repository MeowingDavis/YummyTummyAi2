About Yummy Tummy AI

Meet the Brains Behind the Flavor.

We believe the future of food is personalized, sustainable, and powered by secure tech. Yummy Tummy AI brings together the instincts of professional chefs with the clarity and speed of modern software engineering.

The Origin Story

Professional taste meets secure software.

Yummy Tummy AI started with a simple idea: meal planning should feel as polished as modern software and as helpful as a chef standing beside you. We built it to make weeknight decisions faster, smarter, and less wasteful.

The result is a guided cooking assistant that can turn a messy ingredient list into a clear recommendation, complete with swaps, structure, and momentum.

The TummyBot Philosophy

Transparency

How the AI selects recipes is designed to feel understandable and useful, not like a black box tossing random meals at you.

Sustainability

We focus on reducing food waste through intelligent planning and ingredient-first suggestions that help you cook what you already own.
