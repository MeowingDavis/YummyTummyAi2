About Yummy Tummy AI

Yummy Tummy AI helps you turn the ingredients you have into dinner ideas through a private, guided recipe chat.

What it does

It suggests recipes, ingredient swaps, and step-by-step cooking guidance based on your prompts. Optional account login adds persistent saved chats and profile preferences.

How it works

  1. Type or paste what you have and what you want to cook.
  2. Yummy Tummy AI responds with formatted recipes and alternatives.
  3. Login to keep your saved chats and preferences across sessions.

Privacy

Storage

Saved chats and session context are stored server-side to preserve your conversations.

Data sent

Messages are processed over HTTPS with model providers to generate responses.

Tip: avoid sharing sensitive personal or payment information in prompts.

FAQ

Where are chats stored?⌄

Saved chats are server-side and tied to your account after login.

Does it support photos?⌄

Image attachments are currently not processed.

Is it free?⌄

The app does not charge directly; external model providers may apply their own pricing.
