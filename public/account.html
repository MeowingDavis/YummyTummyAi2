How it works

What users should expect

1. Save once

Enter diets, allergies, and dislikes as short comma-separated lists and save the profile.

2. Chat normally

Future signed-in chats use the saved profile when generating recipe ideas, substitutions, and meal plans.

3. Update anytime

Come back here whenever preferences change. The latest saved profile takes effect immediately.

Input tips

Keep the list clean

  • Use ingredient names, not full sentences.
  • Examples: vegan, gluten-free or peanuts, shellfish, sesame
  • If a recipe conflicts, Yummy Tummy will steer toward safer or better-fitting alternatives.
