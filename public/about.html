About Yummy Tummy AI

Yummy Tummy AI is a lightweight cooking assistant designed to help you come up with meal ideas using whatever ingredients you have at home. It’s built with speed, privacy, and simplicity in mind.

How it Works

  • Enter ingredients or meal ideas in the chat box.
  • The AI suggests recipes, meal combinations, or cooking tips.
  • Keep the conversation going to refine your dish.

Key Features

  • Privacy-friendly — chats are stored in your browser only.
  • Fast & lightweight — built on Deno Deploy with minimal overhead.
  • PWA-ready — installable as an app on desktop or mobile.
  • No clutter — a clean interface focused just on cooking ideas.

Why We Made This

Cooking should be fun and accessible. Instead of scrolling through ads-filled recipe blogs, Yummy Tummy AI gives you quick, focused suggestions tailored to what you have on hand — without distractions.

Try It Out

Head back to the home page and give it a go. Just type in what’s in your fridge and start cooking smarter!
