
Simple Pizza Dough

Make the perfect pizza dough at home with this easy recipe.
Ingredients:

    2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) instant yeast
    1 ½ tsp salt
    1 tsp sugar
    3/4 cup warm water
    1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

    In a large bowl, mix flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.
    Add warm water and olive oil. Stir until combined.
    Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth.
    Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 24 hours.
    Once risen, punch down the dough and roll it out for your pizza.
    Bake with toppings at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes.
