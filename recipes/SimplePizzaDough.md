## Simple Pizza Dough

Make the perfect pizza dough at home with this easy recipe.
<br>
This recipe was written by **Davis Lang.**

---

## Ingredients

- **2 ½ cups** all-purpose flour  
- **1 packet (2 ¼ tsp)** instant yeast  
- **1 ½ tsp** salt  
- **1 tsp** sugar  
- **3/4 cup** warm water  
- **1 tbsp** olive oil  

---

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix **flour**, **yeast**, **salt**, and **sugar**.
2. Add **warm water** and **olive oil**. Stir until combined.
3. Knead the dough for about **5-7 minutes** until smooth.
4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for **24 hours**.
5. Once risen, punch down the dough and roll it out for your pizza.
6. Bake with toppings at **220°C (425°F)** for **12-15 minutes**.

---

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
